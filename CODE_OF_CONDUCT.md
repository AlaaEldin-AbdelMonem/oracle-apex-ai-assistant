# Code of Conduct  
**Oracle APEX AI Assistant — Community Standards**

This project follows a respectful, welcoming, and collaborative community model.  
By participating in this repository, you agree to uphold these guidelines.

---

## 1. Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our project and community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Age  
- Body size  
- Disability  
- Ethnicity  
- Gender identity or expression  
- Level of experience  
- Nationality  
- Personal appearance  
- Race  
- Religion  
- Sexual identity or orientation  

We are committed to fostering an environment of respect, empathy, and constructive dialogue.

---

## 2. Expected Behavior

Participants are expected to:

- Show empathy and respect toward others.  
- Offer constructive feedback.  
- Accept feedback gracefully.  
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.  
- Focus on what is best for the community and project.  
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences.  
- Gracefully accept responsibility for mistakes.

---

## 3. Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.  
- Public or private intimidation or personal attacks.  
- Publishing others’ private information without consent.  
- Disruptive or antagonistic conduct.  
- Any behavior that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

---

## 4. Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior.  
- Taking corrective action when inappropriate behavior occurs.  
- Removing or rejecting contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.  
- Temporarily or permanently banning contributors for harmful actions.

Maintainers are obligated to treat reports of inappropriate behavior with confidentiality and fairness.

---

## 5. Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces  
- GitHub discussions, issues, comments, and pull requests  
- Official community channels  
- Public spaces where individuals represent the project or its community  

---

## 6. Enforcement

Instances of abusive or harmful behavior may be reported by:

- Opening a GitHub issue  
- Contacting project maintainers directly  

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

**Maintainers are obligated to respect the privacy of the reporter.**

---

## 7. Enforcement Guidelines

Depending on the severity and frequency of the violation, maintainers may apply:

1. **Correction** – Formal reminder of the expected behavior  
2. **Warning** – Clear communication about consequences  
3. **Temporary Ban** – Removal from community spaces  
4. **Permanent Ban** – Complete removal from participation
